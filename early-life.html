Kai Suzaan’s Early Life

Kai Suzaan was born into a world already scarred by the Cadmium outbreak. Raised in the Foundry—one of the few surviving fortresses—her early life was shaped by constant vigilance and the hum of machinery.

The Foundry, constructed from the remains of a sprawling industrial complex, provided safety and essential resources amid a landscape overrun by mutated horrors.

The Foundry - a beacon of survival

Her childhood was marked by an ever-present need for caution. Every sound and shadow held the potential threat of Cadmium infection or a sudden attack from mutated monstrosities.

Her parents, famed for their daring explorations into dangerous, infected territories, embarked on one fateful expedition and never returned—leaving Kai with an enduring sense of loss and a fierce determination to understand the virus that changed everything.

Loss fuels her determination

Driven by grief, she immersed herself in studies about the Cadmium virus, learning its biology and seeking any clue that could one day lead to salvation.
